Bunker Hill Community College Housing Information

Housing tips

  • Before committing to a long term place place we encourage you to stay with a host family or at a youth hostel until you are able to find an apartement. It is very expensive to stay at a hotel while you are looking for an apartment
  • Do not sign a lease without seeing the apartment. You must inspect the actual apartment before signing the lease
  • Make sure you read and completely understand the lease before signing it
  • You may consider looking for an apartment outside of Boston. Use the subway Map to help you to locate the twons and areas surrounding Boston. Apartment outside of Boston tend to be less expensive and you can easily commute to Bunker Hill Community College by the "t" (subway). The college is located at the Orange Line Subway- Community College stop. For MBA train stations, bus maps and schedules please check the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (MBTA) website https://www.mbta.com
  • Ask your friends and teachers if they know of anyone who has a room for rent or is looking for a rommate.
